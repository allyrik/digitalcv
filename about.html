Work

I am an employee of the University's Office of Advancement as their student videographer, and I work for Creative Services within the Athletics Department. Both of these positions are helping me grow as an individual, specifically in my videography skills.

In the Office of Advancement, I get the opportunity to create videos in support of Elon Day. This entails editing footage of Dr. Book and past presidents. I get to create content for University wide accounts. Creative Services allows me to attend many sporting events and capture photo and video content of the athletes. I am deeply passionate about storytelling, so these jobs fuel some of my greatest joys!
